### `IsSemigroupGroupPD` (line 19) --- Definition
A function $F : \mathbb{R} \to (\operatorname{Fin}(d) \to \mathbb{R}) \to \mathbb{C}$ is positive-definite on $[0,\infty) \times \mathbb{R}^d$ if for all $n$, coefficients $c$, times $t_i \ge 0$, and spatial points $a_i$:
$$q = \sum_{i,j} \bar{c}_i\, c_j\, F(t_i + t_j,\; a_j - a_i)$$
satisfies $q.\mathrm{im} = 0$ and $0 \le q.\mathrm{re}$.
