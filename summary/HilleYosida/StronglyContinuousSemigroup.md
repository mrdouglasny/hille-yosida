## Overview

Defines strongly continuous one-parameter semigroups ($C_0$-semigroups) on Banach spaces, their infinitesimal generators, the resolvent operator via the Laplace transform, and proves the forward direction of the Hille-Yosida theorem. The key results are: (1) every $C_0$-semigroup has exponential growth $\lVert S(t) \rVert \leq M e^{\omega t}$, (2) the resolvent $R(\lambda)x = \int_0^\infty e^{-\lambda t} S(t)x\, dt$ is a bounded operator with $\lVert R(\lambda) \rVert \leq 1/\lambda$ for contraction semigroups, and (3) the resolvent identity $(\lambda I - A)R(\lambda) = I$.
