## Technical difficulties and solutions

- **`•` right-associativity**: `a • b • c = a • (b • c)`, not `(a • b) • c`.
  Use `← mul_smul` (not `← smul_smul`) to convert `a • (b • c)` to `(a*b) • c`.
- **`smul_eq_mul` for reals**: `a • b = a * b` for `a b : ℝ`, but `rw` doesn't
  see through this. Need explicit `smul_eq_mul` or `mul_smul`.
- **Integral notation precedence**: `∫ t, f t + ∫ t, g t` parses as
  `∫ t, (f t + ∫ t, g t)`. Always parenthesize: `(∫ t, f t) + ∫ t, g t`.
- **`setIntegral_congr` renamed**: Use `setIntegral_congr_fun` in Mathlib 4.28.
- **`integral_add_right_eq_self`**: not found by `lean_local_search` but exists
  in Mathlib (used in Fourier analysis). Found by `grep`.
